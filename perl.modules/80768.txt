Dear Joseph,

I think it's great that you want to submit patches via RT.  Bug
reports with patches are rare and incredibly helpful.  However, PAUSE
ID's are generally only for Perl developers to upload libraries to
CPAN.  (The reason they can also be used on RT is to allow authors to
manage their queues with administrator rights).

For bug reports and patch submission, we encourage people to submit
via email or to register a Bitcard ID instead at
http://www.bitcard.org/
