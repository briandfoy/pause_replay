    This CPAN module provides an interface to access the TextGIS(tm)
    gazetteer, a database for the automatic geo-referencing of textual
    data (toponym resolution, i.e. the assignment of geo-coordinates to
    the place names in text document).

    The approach is described in: Leidner, Jochen L. (2007). Toponym
    Resolution in Text: Annotation, Evaluation and Applications of
    Spatial Grounding of Place Names. Ph.D. thesis, School of
    Informatics, College of Science and Engineering, University of
    Edinburgh, Edinburgh, Scotland, UK.
    http://www.amazon.com/Toponym-Resolution-Text-Annotation-Applications/dp/1581123841

    It is aimed at researchers in geo-spatial text processing that want
    to replicate and build on the methods described in the thesis above,
    and to system developers.
