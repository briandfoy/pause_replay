Vancouver , BC , Canada , November 17th , 2003 -- SMS@ctive Technologies

Recognition(TM). 
Patent pending for Reply Recognition(TM) has been filed in Canada and the
USA under the Patent Corporation Treaty (PCT) which grants the Company
protection in approximately 100 countries. The term "Reply Recognition" is
a registered trademark of the Company. 
Reply Recognition(TM) presents a unique feature in the field of wireless
communications involving Personal Computers (PC), Mainframe Computers and
all the handheld wireless devices. When Reply Recognition(TM) is
commercially implemented it will allow users to communicate from Internet
or wireless based computers to handheld wireless devices and enable mobile
users to communicate with PCs. 

About SMS@ctive 
SMS@ctive's database-driven platform delivers content to mobile users
worldwide while enabling users to capture, access and monitor their mobile
phone information. SMS@ctive specializes in developing innovative premium
wireless content applications, using wireless technology, to fuel business
demand. The Company intends to utilize existing and new wireless network
infrastructures along with novel marketing strategies to increase consumer
awareness ultimately enhancing customer utilization of real-time
information.
