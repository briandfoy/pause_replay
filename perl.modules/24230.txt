  Chess::Board
  Chess-0.5.2 - 25 Sep 2002 - Brian Richardson

  Chess::ICC
  manipulate the Internet Chess Club from the command line.
  Chess-ICC-0.02 - 31 Jul 2002 - Terrence Brannon

  Chess::Mbox
  write mbox files with chess games into them onto disk
  Chess-Mbox-1.3 - 21 Feb 2002 - Terrence Brannon

  Chess::Pgn
  Perl extension for manipulation of chess PGN format. PGN is for Portable
Game Notation and follow the *Portable Game Notation Specification and
Implementation Guide* revised 1994.03.12. You can find i
  Chess-Pgn-0.03 - 27 Dec 2000 - Gilles Maire

  Chess::Piece
  Chess-0.5.2 - 25 Sep 2002 - Brian Richardson

  Chess::Validator
  Chess-0.5.2 - 25 Sep 2002 - Brian Richardson

  Chess::Games::DotCom
  API for accessing chessgames.com
  Chess-Games-DotCom-0.02 - 19 Sep 2003 - Terrence Brannon

  Chess::PGN::EPD
  Perl extension to produce and manipulate EPD text.
  Chess-PGN-EPD-0.21 - 03 Mar 2003 - Hugh S. Myers

  Chess::PGN::Filter
  Perl extension for converting PGN files to other formats.
  Chess-PGN-Filter-0.11 - 28 Jul 2002 - Hugh S. Myers
