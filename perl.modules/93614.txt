morning:

   - Acme-BEWUETHR- Utils-0.02

   - Acme-BEWUETHR- Utils-0.01
   <http://search.cpan.org/~bewuethr/Acme-BEWUETHR-Utils-0.01/>

[ ... ]

Where user BEWUETHER has uploaded four versions of a completely useless
dummy module, to "test" PAUSE functionality.

Notwithstanding that he placed it in Acme:: ... I feel this is a compete
waste and abuse of an important public resource. CPAN being cluttered with
garbage is its one and only serious negative characteristic IMO.

Could there not be ways to discourage this? Better FAQ? Better
documentation? A special CPANTesting:: space or something like that where
the modules are deleted after 24 hours?

I would be willing to help write stuff if there's consensus on this.

- nick

PS I also think there should be a way for module authors to request
complete deletion of a module -- such that it disappears completely from
CPAN, even if it stays in the archives.
