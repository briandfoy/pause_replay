Hands-on Training for Technicians.You’re invited to the Teletraining Institute in Stillwater, Oklahoma for a weeklong Technicians Training program hosted by Professor Scott Baldwin, Department of Engineering Technology at Oklahoma State University.

This program is designed for persons responsible for the day-to-day operation and maintenance of distance learning classrooms and networks.  All sessions of this training will be done using actual working equipment with every participant having the opportunity to work with the equipment and become familiar with its operation.

Participants have opportunities for hands-on work with

·	classroom design and installation
·	networking
·	digital cameras
·	new two-way video systems for the internet
·	audio and video components
·	set up and administer an MCU and other teleconferencing devices
·	computer networking
·	transmission methods
·	troubleshooting strategies

  
http://63.204.221.196/members/response.asp?ID=770203497624
 
 
 
A Message From Your InstructorDear Fellow Technician,

In the past two years I have presented the Technician Training course to several groups of technology professionals.  Quite honestly it is one of the most popular courses that we sponsor each year here at the Institute.

This course is only for technicians VERY SERIOUS about their professional development in distance education.  It focuses on developing resources for the fast paced/fast-changing world of eLearning.  This course is a must for technology professionals, and I look forward to your participation.

Scott Baldwin
Oklahoma State University
Department of Engineering Technology


Technician Training has limited enrollment due to its hands-on nature.  A number of slots have been filled, but there are a few remaining.  The price for the weeklong training is $1795.  Register now for only $897.50 (50% off) if you pay by November 15, 2002.


PS Technician Training is only offered two times each year.  Classes fill up quickly so don’t hesitate to register immediately.

  
http://63.204.221.196/members/response.asp?ID=770203497623
