  rationale:

    CPAN includes many Lingua-*-Numbers distributions for various
    natural languages. The Lingua-Conlang-Numbers distribution currently
    includes modules for three constructed languages: Esperanto
    (Lingua::EO::Numbers), Lojban (Lingua::JBO::Numbers), and Toki Pona
    (Lingua::TokiPona::Numbers), as well as an optional interface to all
    of them from the Lingua::Conlang::Numbers module. More languages
    will be released in the future. Instead of releasing individual
    distributions for a dozen or more conlangs, many of which have much
    simpler numbering systems than natural languages, I'm packaging
    these together. There are currently no Lingua::*::Numbers modules
    for constructed languages on CPAN.
