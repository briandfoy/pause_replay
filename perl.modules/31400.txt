   Lingua::EN::Words2Nums (goes the other way)
   Lingua::EN::WordsToNumbers (goes the other way)
   Lingua::ZH::Numbers
   Lingua::IT::Numbers
   Lingua::DE::Num2Word
   Lingua::CS::Num2Word
   Lingua::ES::Numeros  (now that's refreshing!)
