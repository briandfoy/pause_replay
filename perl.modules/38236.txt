  rationale:

    SVG has a particuar coordinate system for positionning entities,
    which has its origin at the top left corner of the drawing space.
    For graphs and data drawings, it is traditional to have the axis
    origin at the bottom left corner. This module provides a co-ordinate
    transformation utility to support this requirement and keep the
    user's coordinate system clear and easy to work with. It is roughly
    inspired by the paperspace/modelspace separation found within the
    autocad (TM) concept.
