Greg is owner of the namespace. He needs to pass it over to you by
visiting PAUSE and going through the "Change Permissions" menu. You
can upload before he does that but your module will not be indexed.
You can then pick the reindex option on PAUSE after Greg has passed
the ownership over to you.
