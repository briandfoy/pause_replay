    This module provides a dynamic version of HTML::PopupTreeSelect. By
    dynamic I mean that the tree is sent to the client in chunks as the
    user clicks around the tree. In HTML::PopupTreeSelect the entire
    tree is sent to the client when the page is loaded, introducing a
    long delay for large trees. With HTML::PopupTreeSelect::Dynamic
    trees of virtually any size can be navigated without noticable
    delays.
