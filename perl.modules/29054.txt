BC05 	Crisis Command Centre Management 			24Mar  21Jun  20Sep  15Nov
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
The crisis command centre is the core of operations, 
the nerve centre and heart of a business in time of a 
crisis. The command centre will be the focal point for 
the control and response to an emergency within a 
company and for the implementation of recovery 
operations.

This workshop is designed to provide the knowledge 
and practical tools to design, test and implement your 
own command centre.
