Dear DengFeng,

You do not need a PAUSE ID to submit comments and participate on RT.
You can use any OpenID provider (see http://openid.net/get-an-openid/

PAUSE ID's are intended for those submitting modules to CPAN and not
as a general identity service for Perl-related web sites.  RT is
special in having a PAUSE login option for authors so that they can
manage the issue trackers for their modules.

If you do decide to become a CPAN submitter someday, we'd love to
reconsider your application for an ID.
