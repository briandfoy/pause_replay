    NIST uses the SHA Validation System (SHAVS) to check both BIT and
    BYTE implementations of the Secure Hash Algorithms. The SHAVS
    specifies particular formats for short, long, and
    pseudorandomly-generated test messages and their expected digest
    values. NIST uses the formats to document the large number of test
    cases on their site. The Crypt::SHAVS module will give Perl
    programmers the ability to read the formats and to automate the
    checking of their SHA implementations. Moreover, the capabilities
