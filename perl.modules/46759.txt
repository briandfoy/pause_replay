please use the "Register Namespace" form on the pause.perl.org site. 
That way, the maintainers can approve your namespace easily.

If you don't have a PAUSE ID yet, you can register that on the same 
page, too. Once you have an ID, you are ready to upload distributions to 
PAUSE (and thus CPAN) also via pause.perl.org. The namespace 
registration is a separate process for inclusion in the official modules 
list.
